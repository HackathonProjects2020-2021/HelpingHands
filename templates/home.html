    How do I get started?

    Good News: Helping Hands is very easy to use!

    If you're in need of help with anything (lawn mowing, snow shoveling, tutoring, etc.), simply publish your job with the specific information, so volunteers can find them! Confirm that you'd like them to come, and your work will be finished before you know it!

    If you're looking for oppurtunities to volunteer for others, simply go to the list of published jobs and find one that suits you. Reach out to the organizer, and once your application is confirmed, you can get working!
